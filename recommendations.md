# Business Recommendations

## 1. Funnel Optimization

The funnel analysis shows how users move from page view to purchase.

Recommendation: identify the funnel step with the biggest drop-off and focus optimization efforts there first.

## 2. Traffic Source Strategy

Traffic sources should not be evaluated only by visitor volume.

Recommendation: prioritize traffic sources that generate actual purchases, not only page views.

## 3. Time-to-Conversion

The time-to-conversion analysis shows how long converted users take to move from first visit to purchase.

Recommendation: use retargeting or email follow-up for users who do not purchase immediately.

## 4. Revenue Efficiency

Revenue metrics such as average order value and revenue per visitor help evaluate the quality of acquisition.

Recommendation: compare acquisition costs with average order value to avoid spending too much on low-profit customers.
